Frequently Asked Questions

How do I accept a load?
Browse available loads in the "Loads" section, select a load that matches your vehicle and route, and tap "Accept Load". You'll receive confirmation and can start tracking the load immediately.
How do I get paid?
Payments are processed automatically after load completion. You can withdraw funds from your wallet to your bank account. Add your bank details in the "Bank Account" section to enable withdrawals.
How do I add my vehicles?
Go to "My Vehicles" in your profile, tap the "+" button, and fill in your vehicle details including type, registration, capacity, and features. This helps us match you with suitable loads.
How does location sharing work?
Enable location sharing in the "Share Location" section to allow suppliers to track your vehicle in real-time. This helps build trust and can lead to more load opportunities.
What if I need to cancel a load?
Contact the supplier through the Messages section to discuss cancellation. Early cancellations are usually accepted, but late cancellations may affect your rating. Always communicate with the supplier first.
How do I update my profile?
Go to "Profile" and tap "Edit Profile" to update your personal and company information. Changes are saved automatically and sync across all devices.
