Alpha Freight Privacy and Data Protection:

Overview:
- Alpha Freight Solutions Limited collects and processes personal data to operate its freight brokerage platform, support supplier and carrier activity, process payments, improve services, and meet legal obligations
- The privacy policy applies to website visitors, suppliers, carriers, customers, and users of related services

Information collected:
- Personal information may include:
  full name, email address, phone number, physical address, company name, business registration details, billing details, payment method details, and account identifiers

- Carrier-related information may include:
  driver details, vehicle details, license information, insurance information, route activity, and transport-related records

- Supplier-related information may include:
  cargo details, shipment details, pickup and delivery information, booking details, and load-related records

- Technical information may include:
  IP address, device information, browser type and version, pages visited, time spent on pages, interaction data, cookies, and similar tracking technologies

How Alpha Freight uses information:
- To provide freight brokerage and logistics platform services
- To match suppliers with suitable carriers
- To process bookings, transactions, and payments
- To communicate service updates, account messages, and support responses
- To improve website performance, reliability, and user experience
- To detect fraud, abuse, and security risks
- To comply with legal, regulatory, and contractual requirements

How information may be shared:
- With service providers that help operate the platform
- With business partners such as suppliers and carriers where sharing is necessary to deliver the requested service
- With payment processors and supporting transaction partners
- With authorities or legal bodies when disclosure is required by law, regulation, court order, or legal process
- During a merger, acquisition, restructuring, financing event, or asset sale where business data may lawfully transfer

Security:
- Alpha Freight states that it uses reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect personal information
- These protections are intended to reduce the risk of unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction
- No internet-based storage or transmission method is guaranteed to be 100 percent secure

User privacy rights:
- Subject to applicable law, users may have rights to:
  access their data, correct inaccurate data, request deletion, request portability, object to certain processing, and request restriction of processing

- These rights may be available under privacy frameworks such as:
  GDPR, CCPA, and other applicable privacy laws

Cookies and tracking:
- The privacy policy works together with the separate cookie policy
- Cookies and similar tracking technologies may be used for authentication, analytics, preferences, security, and service improvement

Data retention:
- Alpha Freight keeps personal information only for as long as necessary to provide services, resolve disputes, maintain required business records, prevent fraud, and comply with legal or regulatory duties

International transfers:
- Personal information may be processed or stored in countries outside the user's local jurisdiction
- Alpha Freight states that appropriate safeguards are used where required for international transfers

Children's privacy:
- Alpha Freight services are not intended for children under 18
- The company does not knowingly collect personal data directly from children under 18

Account deletion requests:
- Users may request deletion of their account and associated personal data
- Deletion requests can be submitted by emailing support@alphafreightuk.com
- Recommended subject line:
  Account deletion request

- A deletion request should include:
  registered email or phone number, account role, and account ID if available

- Information that may be deleted:
  profile information, contact details, and account identifiers

- Information that may still need to be retained:
  legal records, accounting records, dispute resolution records, fraud prevention records, regulatory compliance records, invoices, and payment history
