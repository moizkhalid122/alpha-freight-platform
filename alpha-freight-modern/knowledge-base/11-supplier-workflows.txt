Alpha Freight Supplier Workflows

Supplier overview
As a supplier (shipper), your job is to publish clear loads, choose the right carrier offer, and keep the shipment moving on time.

Post a Load page (Supplier)
Post a Load is where you create a shipment listing for carriers. The goal is to remove ambiguity so carriers can price accurately and pickup happens smoothly.

How to post a load step by step
1) Enter pickup and delivery locations and appointment windows.
2) Add load details: commodity/load type, weight, equipment requirement, and any special handling notes.
3) Set your offered rate or budget range.
4) Publish the load so carriers can bid or match.

My Posts page (Supplier)
My Posts is where you manage the loads you have posted. The goal is to keep details accurate and monitor progress until the load is booked and delivered.

How to use My Posts step by step
1) Open a post to review pickup/delivery details, timing, and notes.
2) Check carrier activity and offers (often via My Bids).
3) Edit the post if timing, locations, or requirements change.
4) Once booked, follow progress with tracking.

My Bids page (Supplier)
My Bids is where you compare carrier offers. The goal is to pick the best carrier for reliability and timing, not only the lowest or highest price.

How to review bids step by step
1) Compare offers by rate, carrier quality/verification, and pickup readiness.
2) Check equipment fit and whether the carrier can meet your appointment windows.
3) Accept the best bid when it matches timing and reliability.
4) Move into execution and tracking immediately after booking.

Tracking basics (Supplier)
Tracking is how you see if pickup happened, whether the load is moving on time, and if delivery risk is building. The core flow is pickup confirmed → in transit updates → delay alerts → ETA updates → delivery confirmed (POD).

If a supplier asks about “Available Loads”
“Available Loads” is typically a carrier-side page where carriers browse and bid on loads. On the supplier side, you usually work in Post a Load, My Posts, and My Bids.
