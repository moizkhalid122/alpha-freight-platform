Alpha Freight White-Label Platform:

Overview:
- Alpha Freight offers a white-label freight platform for businesses that want to launch their own branded logistics or freight brokerage service
- The platform is web-based and designed so clients can operate under their own brand, domain, and customer relationship
- The goal is to help a business launch a freight platform quickly without building the full system from scratch

Core white-label features:
- Custom branding for logo, colors, typography, and platform appearance
- Multi-tenant platform structure for separate client environments
- AI load matching and freight workflow tools
- Real-time tracking and visibility tools
- Stripe-based payment processing
- API access and webhooks for integrations where supported
- Client dashboard for branding, billing, analytics, team access, and API management

Included modules:
- Load board for posting and managing loads
- Carrier onboarding for inviting carriers, profile management, and verification flow
- Real-time tracking with customer-facing visibility support
- Payments with billing history, invoices, setup fees, subscriptions, and secure Stripe handling
- Analytics and exports for business performance, operations, and finance reporting
- Branding studio for domain, logo, colors, and customer-facing identity
- API and webhooks for CRM, ERP, and internal workflow integrations
- Teams and roles for admin, operations, finance, and access control management

White-label launch flow:
- The client chooses a plan
- The client sends logo, branding assets, and domain requirements
- Alpha Freight sets up the platform and branding, typically within 3-5 business days
- The client goes live with their own freight platform
- In some sales messaging, the platform is promoted as going live in around 7 days

Post-signup flow:
- Client dashboard access or login credentials are typically provided within 24 hours of signup
- The client can configure branding, upload logo assets, choose colors, and set domain preferences
- Alpha Freight completes customization and setup in roughly 3-5 business days
- After launch, the client can invite users, post loads, manage operations, and run the platform under their own brand

White-label support model:
- Alpha Freight supports the white-label platform owner, not the end users of that owner's platform
- This keeps the owner's direct relationship with their own customers intact
- Priority support and stronger onboarding are available on higher plans
- Enterprise plans may include dedicated onboarding, clear escalation paths, and uptime commitments

Migration and onboarding:
- Existing users can be migrated to the platform
- Existing loads and carrier data can also be migrated
- Migration tools and support may be provided to help clients move from an old system

Branding and domain rules:
- The white-label platform is designed so customers see the client's brand, not Alpha Freight branding
- Starter plan generally uses a subdomain such as client.alphafreightuk.com
- Pro and Enterprise plans support a custom domain such as the client's own domain
- Alpha Freight can handle DNS configuration for custom domain setup
- Custom domain and SSL support are part of the stronger white-label offer

Pricing plans:
- Starter plan:
  setup fee around 3000 GBP
  monthly fee around 299 GBP
  includes basic load board, carrier matching, logo and branding, and subdomain access
  does not include API access or a custom domain

- Pro plan:
  setup fee around 5000 GBP
  monthly fee around 599 GBP
  includes everything in Starter plus API access, custom domain support, priority support, and up to 5 user accounts

- Enterprise plan:
  setup fee around 10000 GBP
  monthly fee around 999 GBP
  includes everything in Pro plus dedicated server or dedicated environment, white-glove onboarding, SLA support, and unlimited users

Plan comparison details:
- Load board and carrier matching are included across all plans
- Branding basics are included across all plans
- Custom domain is included in Pro and Enterprise
- API access and webhooks are included in Pro and Enterprise
- Team account limits typically scale from 1 user in Starter to 5 users in Pro to unlimited in Enterprise
- Dedicated environment is an Enterprise-level feature
- SLA and stronger onboarding are mostly Enterprise-level benefits

Security and enterprise operations:
- White-label environments are described as secure-by-design with tenant separation and strict access boundaries
- Stripe is used so sensitive card data stays off the client platform servers
- Role-based access and activity tracking help support accountability across operations and finance teams
- Backups and recovery workflows are positioned as part of reliability planning
- Integrations should use APIs and webhooks for safer automation with CRM or ERP systems

Common client questions:
- Setup usually takes 3-5 business days
- Pro and Enterprise clients can use their own custom domain
- Alpha Freight does not directly support the white-label client's users
- Existing users, loads, and carrier data can be migrated with support
