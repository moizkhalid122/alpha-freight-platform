Operational platform overview:
- Alpha Freight connects suppliers/shippers and carriers through one logistics platform
- The platform focuses on AI load matching, real-time visibility, verified network quality, and smoother freight execution
- Dashboard sections are role-based, so suppliers and carriers see tools relevant to their workflow
- Core supplier tools include posting loads, managing posts, reviewing bids, and controlling payment flow
- Core carrier tools include finding available loads, managing fleet activity, tracking earnings, and reviewing payout-related information

Pricing guidance:
- Pricing is influenced by route distance, timing, cargo type, weight, volume, equipment requirements, and special handling needs
- Suppliers can review pricing inputs before publishing a load
- Budget ranges can be used during load posting to keep shipment pricing aligned with business expectations
- Competitive pricing is part of the supplier-side platform value

Verification and trust:
- Alpha Freight uses a carrier verification process to improve reliability, trust, and shipment confidence
- Verification references include insurance checks, safety review, and performance-based screening
- Verified network quality is a core part of the platform value for both suppliers and carriers

Support and service handling:
- Support is available through live chat, email, and phone channels
- Shipment-linked support can include contextual information such as route or shipment status
- Support interactions are intended to be secure, and support-related business data is treated as protected operational information
