Alpha Freight Cookie Policy and Privacy Controls:

Overview:
- Alpha Freight Solutions Limited uses cookies and similar tracking technologies on its website and mobile applications
- This policy explains what cookies are, how they are used, and what choices users have regarding cookies

What cookies are:
- Cookies are small text files placed on a user's device such as a computer, tablet, or mobile phone
- Cookies help websites work more efficiently and can also provide information to the website owner
- Cookie types can include session cookies, persistent cookies, first-party cookies, and third-party cookies

Cookie categories used by Alpha Freight:
- Essential cookies:
  used for authentication, login sessions, security, fraud prevention, website performance, load balancing, and saving important preferences

- Functional cookies:
  used for language preferences, currency or regional settings, dashboard preferences, and notification settings

- Analytics cookies:
  used to understand page visits, user navigation patterns, website performance, and improvement opportunities

- Marketing cookies:
  used for relevant advertisements, measuring campaign effectiveness, and personalizing marketing content

Specific cookie examples:
- carrierAuth / supplierAuth:
  used to store the user authentication session
  duration may be session-based or persistent depending on the login flow

- alphaPreferredLanguage:
  stores the user's language preference
  persistent duration around 1 year

- ab.portal.lastRole:
  remembers the last used portal such as carrier or supplier
  persistent duration around 30 days

Third-party services that may set cookies or similar identifiers:
- Google Analytics for analytics and performance monitoring
- Firebase for authentication and database-related services
- Supabase for storage and backend services
- Payment processors for secure payment handling

User cookie choices:
- Users may manage cookies through browser settings
- Users can block all cookies, block third-party cookies, delete cookies, or ask the browser to notify them when cookies are set
- Blocking essential cookies may reduce or break website functionality
- Users may also interact with a cookie consent banner where available
- Users may accept all cookies, reject non-essential cookies, or customize preferences
- Some third-party services such as Google Analytics may provide their own opt-out tools

Mobile app equivalents:
- Mobile applications may use local storage for user preferences
- Mobile applications may use device identifiers for analytics
- Mobile applications may use push notification tokens
- These controls can often be managed through device settings or in-app preferences

Updates and notifications:
- Alpha Freight may update the cookie policy from time to time
- Material changes may be communicated by updating the page, changing the last updated date, or sending email or in-app notices for significant changes

International compliance:
- Cookie laws may vary by country or jurisdiction
- Alpha Freight states that it complies with GDPR, CCPA, and other applicable privacy laws
- User cookie preferences are intended to be respected globally
