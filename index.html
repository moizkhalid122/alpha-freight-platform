Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about payment, carrier verification, and international freight

How does payment work on Alpha Freight?

Alpha Freight offers flexible payment options. Suppliers pay per load with transparent pricing—no hidden fees or monthly commitments. Carriers receive payments within 7 days of successful delivery. We support all major payment methods including bank transfers, credit cards, and digital wallets.

How do you verify carriers?

All carriers undergo a comprehensive verification process including license verification, insurance checks, vehicle documentation, and background screening. We maintain a network of verified carriers with ratings and reviews from previous shipments to ensure reliability and quality service.

Do you handle international freight?

Yes, Alpha Freight handles international freight across Europe and beyond. We manage customs documentation, cross-border regulations, and coordinate with international carriers. Our platform supports shipments to and from the UK, EU, and select international destinations with full tracking and compliance support.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major payment methods including Visa, Mastercard, American Express, PayPal, bank transfers, and digital wallets. Payments are processed securely and suppliers can choose their preferred payment method when posting loads.

How long does carrier verification take?

Carrier verification typically takes 24-48 hours. We verify licenses, insurance, vehicle documentation, and conduct background checks. Once verified, carriers can immediately start accepting loads on our platform.

What countries do you support for international freight?

We support international freight to and from the UK, all EU countries, and select destinations worldwide. Our platform handles customs clearance, documentation, and compliance requirements for cross-border shipments. Contact us for specific country availability.
