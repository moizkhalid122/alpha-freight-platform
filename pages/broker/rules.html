Broker Rules & Terms

Comprehensive guidelines and regulations for all Alpha Freight brokers. Please read carefully before joining our platform.

1. BROKER ELIGIBILITY

  • Must be UK registered business/sole trader
  • Minimum 1 year logistics experience
  • Valid business bank account required
  • Company registration proof (Companies House)
  • VAT number if turnover exceeds £85,000
  • Clean legal/financial history

2. COMMISSION STRUCTURE

  • Platform Fee: 5% of your earned commission
  • Your Commission Range: 8% minimum, 20% maximum
  • Commission Charged Only on successful deliveries
  • Platform fee deducted automatically
  • Weekly payouts every Friday
  • Minimum withdrawal: £100

3. PAYMENT RULES

  • ALL payments must go through platform escrow
  • NO direct payments to carriers allowed
  • Suppliers pay to platform before load assignment
  • Carriers paid within 7 days of proof upload
  • Late payments incur 2% weekly interest
  • Disputed payments held max 14 days

4. LOAD POSTING RULES

  • Accurate load details mandatory
  • No fake/inflated loads permitted
  • Competitive pricing required
  • Real-time status updates mandatory
  • Maximum 2% cancellation rate allowed
  • Cancellation fee: £10 per extra cancellation

5. CARRIER SELECTION

  • Must use platform-verified carriers
  • Can add own carriers after verification
  • Check carrier ratings before assignment
  • Insurance verification for loads > £5,000
  • Your rating affected by carrier disputes

6. CLIENT MANAGEMENT

  • You own client relationships
  • Platform provides tools only
  • Client data confidential (GDPR compliant)
  • Non-poaching: Can't approach platform's direct users
  • Can onboard existing clients

7. DISPUTE RESOLUTION

Level 1: Platform automated mediation

24 hours

Level 2: Human review

48 hours

Level 3: Independent arbitration

Cost shared 50/50

Level 4: UK court jurisdiction

Legal proceedings

Platform decision final for disputes under £5,000

8. RATING SYSTEM

  • Mutual ratings after each transaction
  • Ratings affect search priority
  • Below 3.5/5 → Warning
  • Below 3.0/5 → 7-day suspension
  • Below 2.5/5 → Permanent ban
  • Fake reviews → Immediate suspension

9. SUBSCRIPTION TERMS

  • Monthly/Annual billing
  • Auto-renew unless cancelled 7 days before
  • Downgrade anytime, upgrade immediate
  • No refunds for partial months
  • 14-day free trial available

10. PLATFORM USAGE

  • One account per business
  • No bots/automation allowed
  • No data scraping
  • 80% minimum response rate to messages
  • Profile must be updated quarterly
