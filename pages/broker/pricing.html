How We Earn Commission

Our revenue model explained

From Carriers

We charge a small commission from carriers on each successful load delivery. This commission is automatically deducted from the payment when the load is completed.

Example:
If a carrier completes a load worth £1,000, we charge 2-5% commission (£20-£50) depending on the load value and carrier tier.

From Suppliers

We charge a transaction fee from suppliers on each load posted and successfully matched. This fee covers platform usage and matching services.

Example:
If a supplier posts a load worth £2,000, we charge 1-3% transaction fee (£20-£60) when the load is successfully matched with a carrier.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do suppliers and carriers need to subscribe?
No! The platform is completely free for Suppliers and Carriers. They don't need to pay any monthly fees. Subscription is only for brokers.
How is commission calculated?
Commission is automatically calculated on every successful transaction. If you're on the Basic plan, it's 2%, on the Pro plan it's 1%, and on the Enterprise plan it's 0.5% commission.
Can I change my plan?
Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes will apply from the next billing cycle.
Is there a trial period?
Yes, you can take a 14-day free trial of any plan. You won't be charged during the trial period.
How do I make payment?
You can pay using credit card, debit card, or bank transfer. Monthly subscription will automatically renew.
When is commission charged?
Commission is charged only on successful transactions. The commission rate depends on your selected subscription plan.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. You can cancel your subscription anytime. Your plan will remain active until the end of your current billing period.
Do you offer onboarding help?
Yes. We can provide onboarding and workflow guidance—especially for Pro and Enterprise customers.
