Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our white-label solution

How long does setup take?

Setup typically takes 3-5 business days. This includes domain configuration, branding integration, and initial platform customization. Enterprise plans may take slightly longer due to additional features.

Can I use my own domain?

Yes! Pro and Enterprise plans include custom domain support. You can use your own domain (e.g., yourdomain.com) instead of the default subdomain. We'll handle all the DNS configuration for you.

Do you provide support to my users?

No, we provide support to you as the platform owner. You'll have access to our support team and resources to help your users. This keeps your brand relationship with your customers intact.

Can I migrate my existing users?

Yes, we provide migration tools and support to help you import your existing user base, loads, and carrier data. Our team will work with you to ensure a smooth transition.
