Profile Completion Guide

Welcome to Alpha Freight! Completing your profile is essential to start receiving and managing load bookings. This process helps us verify your business and ensure secure transactions for all parties involved.

Why Complete Your Profile?

  • Verification: Your profile information is reviewed to ensure compliance with transportation regulations and safety standards.
  • Secure Payments: Bank details are required for secure payment processing and withdrawals. All financial information is encrypted and stored securely.
  • Better Matching: Complete profiles help us match you with the most suitable loads based on your fleet size, experience, and location.
  • Account Activation: Your profile will be activated within 1-24 hours after successful verification by our admin team.

What to Expect:

  • Step 1: Select your business type (Business/Fleet Owner or Local Truck/Owner-Operator)
  • Step 2: Provide company or truck information including address, experience, and fleet details
  • Step 3: Upload documents (optional) (Operator License/Driving License, Vehicle Registration, Insurance)
  • Step 4: Enter bank details for receiving payments securely
  • Step 5: Complete identity verification with passport or ID card

Privacy & Security: All your information is protected using industry-standard encryption. Documents are stored securely and only accessed by authorized personnel for verification purposes. Bank details are used exclusively for payment processing.

Need Help? Use the chatbot icon in the bottom right corner for assistance at any time during the process.
