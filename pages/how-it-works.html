Payment Process with Stripe

Secure, fast, and reliable payments powered by Stripe

Powered by Stripe

How Payments Work

Secure Payment Setup

When a supplier selects your bid, payment is securely held by Stripe until delivery is confirmed. The funds are protected until delivery is completed.

Automated Processing

Once delivery is confirmed and both parties verify completion, payment is automatically released to the carrier's account within 24-48 hours.

Payment Flow:

Carrier Selected
Payment Secured
Stripe Secure Payment Holding
Delivery Confirmed
Payment Released
24-48 hours
Payment Protection

All payments are securely held by Stripe. Suppliers can't cancel payment after delivery confirmation, and carriers are guaranteed payment for completed deliveries.

Fast Transfers

Once delivery is confirmed, payments are processed automatically and transferred to your bank account within 24-48 hours. No manual processing required.

Complete Records

All transactions are recorded and you can download invoices, receipts, and payment history at any time from your dashboard.

Payment Methods Accepted

Stripe accepts all major payment methods:

  • Credit and Debit Cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express)
  • Bank Transfers
  • Apple Pay and Google Pay
  • Direct debit (for recurring payments)

Security & Trust

Your data and payments are protected with industry-leading security

SSL Encryption

All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using 256-bit SSL encryption. Your information is never exposed during transmission.

Identity Verification

All suppliers and carriers are verified before account approval. We verify business registration, licenses, insurance, and contact information to ensure platform safety.

Stripe PCI Compliance

Stripe is PCI DSS Level 1 certified - the highest level of security certification for payment processors. Your card details are never stored on our servers.

Secure Data Storage

All user data is stored on secure, encrypted databases with regular backups. We follow GDPR and international data protection regulations.

Privacy Protection

Your personal and financial information is never shared with third parties without your consent. We maintain strict privacy controls and data access protocols.

Dispute Resolution

In case of any issues, our dedicated support team provides mediation and dispute resolution services. Payments are protected until issues are resolved.

Alpha Freight provides mediation support only and is not a party to the transport contract between suppliers and carriers.

What Makes Our Payments Secure?
  • Stripe secure payment holding: Payments are securely held by Stripe until delivery is confirmed, protecting both parties.

  • Secure API: All payment processing uses Stripe's secure API - card details never touch our servers.
  • Regular Audits: Our security is regularly audited and updated to meet industry standards.
