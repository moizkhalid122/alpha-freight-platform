About This Course

The Certificate of Professional Competence (CPC) is a mandatory qualification for professional drivers in the UK. This comprehensive course covers all essential topics required to pass your CPC assessment and maintain compliance with UK regulations.

Our structured curriculum is designed by industry experts and includes practical examples, real-world scenarios, and interactive assessments to ensure you're fully prepared.

Curriculum

1
Driver CPC Rules & Regulations
Understand the legal framework, driver responsibilities, and key regulations governing professional driving in the UK. Learn about licensing requirements, periodic training obligations, and compliance standards.
2
Working Time Directive
Master the rules around driving hours, rest periods, and working time limits. Learn how to properly record and manage your working hours to stay compliant and safe.
3
Digital Tachographs
Comprehensive training on digital tachograph operation, data recording, and analysis. Learn to use tachograph cards, interpret data, and maintain proper records.
4
Driver Safety & Well-being
Essential safety protocols, defensive driving techniques, and health management for professional drivers. Learn to identify and manage fatigue, stress, and health risks.
5
Emergency First Aid
Practical first aid training for road incidents. Learn life-saving techniques, emergency response procedures, and how to assist at accident scenes.
6
Final Assessment
Comprehensive assessment covering all course modules. Upon successful completion, you'll receive your CPC certificate and Academy badge.
