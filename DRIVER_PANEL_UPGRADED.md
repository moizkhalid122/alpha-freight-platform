## 📱 How to Use:

### For Carrier:
1. Open any carrier page
2. Click "Share Location" in sidebar
3. Driver panel opens in new tab
4. Send link to driver

### For Driver:
1. Open driver panel
2. Enter Driver ID
3. Click "Start Sharing"
4. Allow location access
5. Watch status turn green!
